About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles, supporting regulated and high-consequence sectors. We bridge leading UK academic research with project-based learning and hands-on experience, equipping the workforce to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our academic partnership ensures that every program reflects the latest insight, while our industry collaborations deliver real value on UK and global projects.
