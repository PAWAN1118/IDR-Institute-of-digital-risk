Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Brand & Homepage Design
Project Overview
This project presents the branding and homepage design for the Institute of Digital Risk (IDR), an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk.
The goal of this project is to design a minimalist technology-focused brand identity and develop a responsive single-page website that clearly communicates IDR’s mission, services, and community.
The homepage explains how IDR trains digital risk professionals, connects academic knowledge with industry practice, and supports organizations operating in regulated and high-consequence environments.
Features
1. Logo Design
The IDR logo follows a modern, minimal design approach suitable for technology and education institutions.
Key Characteristics
Cube-inspired geometric icon representing structure, stability, and risk management.
Color palette:
Orange – innovation, energy, and forward thinking
Black – professionalism and authority
White – clarity and simplicity
Two logo variants
Icon-only version (for favicon, social media, mobile header)
Icon + text version ("Institute of Digital Risk")
Scalable format suitable for small displays and high-resolution screens.
Design Explanation
The cube-inspired shape symbolizes structured risk management and resilience in digital systems. The orange color reflects innovation in emerging technologies and AI governance, while black and white maintain a professional and trustworthy identity suitable for a training institute. The typography is clean and modern to emphasize clarity, accessibility, and a technology-focused brand image.
